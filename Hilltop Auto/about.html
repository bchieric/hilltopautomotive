About

Hilltop Automotive was founded in 1973 at the peak of the automotive "Golden Years".
Since the 70's our company has thrived on helping individuals with all of their automotive needs.
Whether you need an oil change or a new engine we are here to help!
Our towing services are available weekly 24/7 through both our partners at AAA New England
as well as our firefighters and the New Hampshire State Police.
Thank you for choosing us to be your most trusted name in automotive.
